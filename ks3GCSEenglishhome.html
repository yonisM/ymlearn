Module 1

1. Reading: Word Meaning Not Available

At the end of the lesson, you should be able to understand and explain the meaning of words

2. Reading: Retrieving Information Not Available

At the end of the lesson, you should be able to retrieve and record information and identify key details from the text

3. Reading: Summarising Main Ideas Not Available

At the end of the lesson, you should be able to summarise main ideas from more than one paragraph

4. Reading: Making Inferences Not Available

At the end of the lesson, you should be able to make inferences and justify them with evidence from the text.

5. Reading: Commenting on Structure Not Available

At the end of the lesson, you should be able to explain how different parts of the text are related and describe the function of the part of a text.

6. Reading: Choice of language Not Available

At the end of the lesson, you should be able identify how language can affect meaning.

7. Reading: Comparisons Within Text Not Available

At the end of the lesson, you should be able to make comparisions within texts.
