The appointment explained!

Part 1: Introduction

The YMLearn Tutor will introduce themselves and the Student/Parent will be asked to do the same. The YMLEarn Tutor will then outline the objective of the appointment.

Part 2: YMLearn Induction Test

The Student will be asked to sit the YMLearn's Induction Test. The test will last roughly 45 minutes. The idea of the test is for the YMLearn tutor to work out level the student is currently at. You don't have to wait until the appointment to see how the student is doing. Try our online test today!

Part 3: The Induction Test Result and Tutoring Plan

Once we have established the level the student is at, the YMLearn Tutor will talk you through how we will help the student achieve the best possible grade. This will include us: talking you through:

  • The YMlearn website;
  • The YMlearn mini-booklets;
  • YMLearn quizzes;
  • Our one-to-one tuition;
  • Our Skype Session!
