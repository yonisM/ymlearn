Many Students work incredibly hard to achieve a good grade. However, working hard doesn't mean your child will achieve high grades. Working SMART does. Here at the YM Learn we teach your child to become SMART learners, not just hard workers. By following these 3 steps of SMART learning, we will help your child achieve academic and exam success.


Teach

Here at YM Learn we believe each child is unique! They learn in different ways and they learn at different speeds. We allow your child to work at their own pace with a support from a designated tutor.

Practice

Once your child has grasped the concept, we then give them plenty of opportunities to practice what they have learned. Whilst at the centre, they will work from a printed worksheet with lots of practice questions. Outside the centre, they can practice what they have learned by viewing our online materials and quizzes.

Exam

Once we have taught your child the material and had plenty of practice, we then examine them! By examining your child very often, we give them the confidence and the experience they need to achieve excellent grades in their exam.
